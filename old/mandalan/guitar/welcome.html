Welcome to Alicia Taylor Guitar Studio. Alicia Taylor is a professional guitar soloist with over 20 years experience. She plays a variety of styles including Classical, Flamenco, Latin, Modern, Folk, and much more. She is currently accepting new students and booking performances for the 2009 year. Please listen to a sample of her music at her MySpace page.

Alicia Taylor's Studio is in her home in Christiana, Tennessee. She is willing to travel to events in the Middle Tennessee area including Murfreesboro, Nashville, LaVergne, Franklin and Lebanon. Additional travel is negotiable.
