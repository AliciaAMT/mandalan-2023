Private Lessons

All ages and levels welcome. Styles include Classical, Flamenco, Latin, Acoustic, Rock, Classic Rock, and many more. Lessons also include lessons in reading music and music theory if desired. Alicia provides free learning materials for her students including cd's of studied music recorded by herself as well as sheet music or music in electronic format. There will be a student recital twice a year at which she will also play and invite a special guest artist.

Prices:
$20 half hour
$30 hour

Payments must be made one lesson ahead of time. No refunds for missed lessons. First lesson is $50 and may last up to 2 hours depending on the student. This $50 initial fee includes the first extended lesson at a discount, and a prepaid second lesson. Ask for special discounts for prepaid months or yearly rates.

All materials included except:
Tuner/Metronome
Guitar
Guitar strings
Footstool
Music Stand

Alicia especially enjoys teaching young children since she also began learning the guitar at a young age and can relate to the specific issues that a young student faces. Advanced students will also benefit from Alicia's training and her custom tailored study regimen guaranteed to help improve guitar technique.
