Weddings and Events

Add a special atmosphere to your event with the guitar music of Alicia Taylor. Alicia Taylor is a solo guitarist with over 20 years experience. She has an extensive and diverse repertoire and is willing to learn a special request in advance. Alicia will include a personal consultation for wedding performances.

Styles include Classical, Latin, Flamenco, Pop, Acoustic Fingerstyle and more.

Prices:
Weddings $200
Includes approximately 30 minutes seating music, Processional music, Bridal March, Unity Candle or other special ritual, and recessional. Additional fees may apply for travel over 25 miles, additional music, or special requests. There must be a six week advance notice on special requests. There will be an $80 non refundable deposit due when booking.

Other events begin at $150 per hour at the discretion of the artist.
